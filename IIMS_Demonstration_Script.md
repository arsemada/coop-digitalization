"IIMS is a comprehensive digital platform designed specifically for SACCOs and cooperative unions. It transforms traditional paper-based operations into streamlined, transparent, and efficient digital management."

## Key Features (5 minutes)

### 🏛️ **Core Modules**
- **Member Management**: Digital registration, profiles, and records
- **Financial Operations**: Savings, loans, transactions, and accounting
- **Reporting Dashboard**: Real-time analytics and insights
- **Mobile Access**: USSD, SMS, and web portal for all members

### 🔒 **Security & Compliance**
- Bank-level encryption and data protection
- Audit trails for all transactions
- Regulatory compliance built-in
- Role-based access control
